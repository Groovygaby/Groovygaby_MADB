Fake News.

Begin your learning with this introduction page that gives a fun and interactive taster of what the rest of the website will cover, then delve deeper into the constitution of fake news and the unsettling world of deepfakes in the second tab entitled ‘What is Fake News’. Continue your journey by examining real-life stories where misinformation led to significant consequences, showcasing the importance of being critically aware in the ‘Fake News Stories’ tab. For practical strategies and tools to identify fake news, don't miss the ‘How to Spot Fake News Tab’ where a handbook of essential guidance is provided. Finally, put your knowledge to the test with the interactive quiz in the last tab entitled ‘Fake News Quiz’. Each tab on this website is designed to build your understanding and skills, empowering you to navigate the digital world with awareness and responsibility.
